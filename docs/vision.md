# Vision

## What is PersonaDB?

PersonaDB is a long-term personal data platform that brings together information from the services, applications, and collections I use into a single, unified system.

Rather than relying on disconnected platforms, PersonaDB aims to import, organize, and enrich this data while providing a consistent interface for exploring it.

The project is designed to become the central source of truth for my digital life.

---

## Goals

- Consolidate data from multiple sources into one platform.
- Preserve ownership of my own data.
- Build reusable import pipelines.
- Normalize data into consistent schemas.
- Provide applications and services for browsing, searching, and analysing information.
- Create a platform that can continue to grow over many years.
- Serve as a portfolio project demonstrating modern software engineering practices.

---

## Principles

### Own the data

Where possible, data should be stored locally rather than relying solely on third-party services.

### Import, don't duplicate

External services remain the source of exported data, while PersonaDB is responsible for importing, organizing, and enriching that data.

### Modular architecture

Applications, services, and shared libraries should remain loosely coupled so they can evolve independently.

### Automation first

Imports, updates, and maintenance tasks should be automated wherever practical.

### Long-term maintainability

Design decisions should favour readability, simplicity, and maintainability over unnecessary complexity.

---

## Planned Scope

PersonaDB is expected to support a wide variety of data over time, including:

- Movies
- Television
- Video games
- Achievements and trophies
- Books
- Music
- Board games
- Personal collections
- Dead by Daylight statistics
- Additional sources as the project evolves

---

## Success

PersonaDB succeeds if it becomes the platform I naturally use to explore and understand my own data, while also serving as a showcase of the software engineering skills developed throughout its creation.
